gravity = Ft
Ft = m * g
m = mass
g = gravitational acceleration, g can be found with g = (G * M)/(r²)
G is the Universal Gravitational Constant and is 6.6743 × 10-11 N/(kg2/m2)
M is the mass of the mass of the celestial body assosiated with G
r is distence from the center of the mass to the other point in space

det helle kan skrives som Ft = G*(M*m)/(r²)
